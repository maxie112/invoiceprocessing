The App

Send receipts and invoices via your smartphone

The App

go2UBL offers a free App for the mobile phone with which you can easily take a picture of an invoice or receipt. The document is then converted to a UBL and delivered to the financial administration.

The App is available for iOS and Android.

A short manual for the correct installation and setup of the App can be found on our FAQ-page.
